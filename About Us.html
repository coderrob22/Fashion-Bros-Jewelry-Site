About Us.

The story of how two brothers came together to form the finest jewelry company for the next generation.



Our Story

The tale of two brothers begin with our love of fashion from in our teens. We were into what was new and fresh from very early on. Our friends knew us as the "in boys" we only wore what was fashionable..whether it was clothing or accessories.

Around college was when we took our first business class and decided to combine our love of fashion to what we were learning in "Intro to Business Models 1001" We applied for our first loan and bought the building near our favorite mall.

The store you see here today is an appreciation of all of our dreams and inspirations inspired. This is the culmination of our long, hard nights, many tears, and tireless efforts to accomplishing something we only dreamed of. This is us and we are not at all ashamed of the time, blood, or sweat we put into this business.
